Download & Install App on iOS

Follow these steps to install the app on your iPhone:

  • Go to the App Store;
  • Search for the 20Bet app;
  • Click the Get button;
  • After downloading, the app will be installed automatically and appear on your screen.

For Android devices users, you will need the following:
