How to Install the 20Bet App on Desktop?

In addition to mobile devices, the app can also be installed on your desktop computers. The process is simple - you just need to visit the official website, click the 20Bet App button at the top left of the website, and the icon will appear on your desktop in just a few seconds.

There are no major differences between the browser version and the app. Every feature will also be present inside the app fully.
